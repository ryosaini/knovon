Case Study 1

Our customer is a thought leader in Pharmaceutical Marketing and had developed an interview process and accompanying excel based analytics to visualise the data.

The customer challenge was to build a web based tool to conduct interviews, and automatically compile, anlyse and visualise the data.

The result was a web based application with dashboards developed using Hi-Charts. The web application was developed as per Pharmaceutical IT security standards and is currently deployed across the major pharmaceutical companies.

A UK based Recruitment firm wanted a Web Based solution to visualise their company demographics data. Their objective was to promote their position as leaders in diversity & equality and help expand their offerings to the market.
